Research Projects

I have worked in various research projects at the Norwegian University of Science and Technology (NTNU) from 2018-2023, with close collaboration with companies in Raufoss Industry Park at the Vestre Toten municipality in Norway, including SINTEF Manufacturing AS. My research group was Digital Technologies.
SFI Manufacturing
Researcher at NTNU - Centre of Excellence for Manufacturing & Automation. Developed a vision-based quality inspection system for the Cyber-Physical (CP) Learning Factory in collaboration with SINTEF Manufacturing AS, Norway. Dealt with big data inside the CP factory, using ML/AI to predict and maintain system performance.NTNU, Norway - 2018-2023
EV Powertrain
Collaborative research with researchers from IIT Kharagpur on Multi-objective Optimization of the Powertrain of Electric Vehicles (EV). Conducted during PostDoc tenure at NTNU, Norway.NTNU Norway & IIT Kharagpur - 2019-2022
Decision Support
Expert researcher in building mathematical models to aid Decision-Support Systems (DSS) and Multi-Criteria Decision-Making (MCDM) tools for industrial applications.
