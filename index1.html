I am Tamal Ghosh an associate researcher and data scientist, working in NTNU i Gjøvik for last 5 years. I am interested in industrial research, where academic knowledge can be extended to improve the product development process in the industry. My approach is to adopt robust design wherever and whenever possible and optimize the whole process. The Use of machine learning is an important part of my research as it involves a huge amount of data being analyzed inside production units. My current interest also lies in digital twin research, as it can also be used to validate the production process before being used in real-time. The diagram underneath shows how my research is connected between the digital and physical worlds. I am also an expert researcher in building mathematical models to aid Decision-Support Systems, Multi-Criteria Decision-Making tools. I have expertise in developing and implementing evolutionary algorithms, nature-inspired optimization, and deep learning tools and techniques. I am an avid computer programmer. I also have worked as a faculty of IT/AI courses at different levels (diploma/PG) for 5 years. I like working with like-minded people.



If any MS/PhD candidate is interested in working with me as a visiting researcher or wants to do his/her thesis work, drop me an email.
